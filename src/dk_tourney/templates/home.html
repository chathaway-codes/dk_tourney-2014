Digital Khaos 2014

March 1st, 2014

Digital Khaos is an annual LAN party hosted by Vermont Technical College. It is run entirely by a student organization known as "The Adventures Guild". All the organization, networking, software development, and marketing is done by students in the club.

Last year, we had over 150 students show up for the whole 2-day event. We invite all Vermont Tech students to come this year, and bring a friend! If you want to come, but you're not a student and don't know any students, shoot an email to HPhan@vtc.vsc.edu. We'll see if we can fit you in!

Happy gaming!
