Digital Khaos is an annual LAN party hosted by Vermont Technical College. It is run entirely by a student organization known as "The Adventures Guild". All the organization, networking, software development, and marketing is done by students in the club.

Last year, we had over 150 students show up for the whole 2-day event. We invite all Vermont Tech students to come this year, and bring a friend! If you want to come, but you're not a student and don't know any students, shoot an email to HPhan@vtc.vsc.edu. We'll see if we can fit you in!

Where is it?

This year, DK is being held in Judd on Vermont Tech's Randolph campus. Randolph campus is at:

Vermont Technical College
1 Main St
Randolph Center, VT 05061-0500

Judd is pretty easy to find after you drive in. Park in the first parking lot you see, go into the courtyard, and it's on the otherside of the bookstore.

When is it?

We will be setting up Friday night, Februrary 28th. The gaming begins March 1st, early-ish (we're aiming for 8AM). Things should be moving by noon. The plan is to go all night, and end around 5PM on Sunday, the 2nd.

What should I bring?

There is no entrance fee, but you may want some cash for snacks. You are of course welcome to bring your own food, but do NOT bring anything alcoholic! If you are going to be playing computer games, bring:

  • Your computer
  • A monitor, if you need one
  • Cables for your computer (including ethernet)
  • A power strip, just in case

If you are just playing console games, and feel like bringing your console, please do. We will need people to help us get enough consoles for the tournaments, as we only have a few. TV's will also be in short supply.
